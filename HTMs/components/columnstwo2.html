The Scrolls of Āryavarta

Dhārmika lore, narrative and fiction- catering to our cultural yearnings for a Bhārata that once-was, and some of those that could-have-been.

Scrolls of Āryavarta is a project combining AI-rendered art with narrative storytelling to take us beyond time to a places we've all been to- but not in this lifetime.

The first chapter is a collaboration with The Emissary that takes us into the mind of a ṛṣi with a familiar name.
